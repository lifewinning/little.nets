A CSprite, is an ordered list of these elements, created with <CTRL-r>.  
Normally, they are packed together in a list and the address of the first is 
passed to routines.

See ::/Demo/Graphics/SpritePlot.CPP, ::/Demo/Graphics/SpritePlot3D.CPP, 
::/Demo/Graphics/SpritePut.CPP and SpriteMeshEd().

Be aware that copying SP, IB, or IS entries with the clipboard results in 
duplicate entries with different nums.  You can manually remove dups by editing 
with <CTRL-t> and setting to the original num.

See ::/Adam/Gr/GrSpritePlot.CPP for how CSprite are stored.
